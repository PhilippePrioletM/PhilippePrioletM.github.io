About Me

Welcome to my website! I am Philippe Priolet, an astronomer and astrophysicist specializing in the study of exozodiacal dust.

I have a passion for unraveling the mysteries of exozodiacal dust and its implications for understanding planetary systems beyond our own. My research focuses on using advanced observational techniques and modeling to investigate the properties, distribution, and origins of exozodiacal dust clouds.

Through my work, I aim to contribute to our understanding of planetary formation and evolution, the potential habitability of exoplanets, and the search for extraterrestrial life.
